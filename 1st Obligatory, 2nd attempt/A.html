Assignment module 1: The interplay between technology and society

This assignment counts as 1 of the 8 possible obligatory assignments in this course. Remember that you have to pass 5 of them to qualify for the exam. Assignment 0, on group work, is not optional, but must be completed by all groups.

Step:

1. Download the attached document containing the assignment and the assignment form.
2. Save the document. Include your group number in the title of the file.
3. Familiarize yourself with the assignment and assignment form before your group discussions in the lab.
4. Read the assignment instructions in the document. Use the assignment form in the document to guide your discussions. Fill in the form as your discussions progress. Save as Word file. Only one member of the group needs to submit in Canvas.
